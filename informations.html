Frequently Asked Questions

What is a trade wishlist?
A trade wishlist is a list where you can add items you wish to trade with others. It helps you keep track of what you're looking for and facilitates trading with others.
How do I create a trade wishlist?
To create a trade wishlist, go to the wishlist page in the sidebar menu. Once in, you can start adding stickers by searching for them and selecting the ones you want to trade then you take a screenshot and you are good to go.
How do i add stickers to my wishlist?
You can add stickers to your wishlist by clicking on the "Add Items" button and searching for them using keywords (like stickers titles, attack, convert rate, tools, signs, puffshrooms, stamps etc.) and then selecting the stickers from the search results.
What if i change my mind or make a mistake while adding stickers?
You can easily remove items from your wishlist at any time. Simply go to your wishlist, find the sticker you want to remove, and click on it to delete it.
How secure is my personal information on this website?
We do not collect any informations, the website is cookies free and your connection is encrypted.
